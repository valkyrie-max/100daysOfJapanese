100 Days of Japanese: Challenge!

Many people in the tech community are familiar or heard about the 100 Days of Code challenge. I, as one of those individuals, got interested in the challenge and tried to do it several times. Unfortunately, different life circumstances would prevent me from completing it.

However! I've recently finished my studies and I've started to code or learn something new about coding literally everyday. That said, my everyday life turned into the #100DaysOfCode challenge. Which sound really cool right? But what if I tell you that I am so obsessed with constant self-improvement that even during my free time/weekends I still continue to learn something new?

So, thanks to the 100 Days of Code challenge, I've decided to document here the 100 Days of Japanese challenge that I've made up for myself to see where it will take me! I feel like it's a cool idea to combine my coding skills with passion and love for Japan and its culture.

TLDR; Join me on the journey of me learning Japanese language inspired by 100 Days of Code challenge!

Important notice: I do not claim that I started any of the challenges. I only claim whatever I am/will be posting here. Enjoy!
